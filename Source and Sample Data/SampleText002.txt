Author: Jules Verne
Time: 2AM

Text:"A young woman, alone in her apartment at 2AM, hears a knock on her door. She cautiously opens it, and finds a man standing there, asking for directions. 
She tells him where he needs to go, and he thanks her before walking away. The woman is curious about the man, and decides to follow him. 
She follows him all the way to a derelict building, where she sees him enter a dark room.
She decides to leave, but as she's walking away, she hears a loud noise coming from the building. 
She turns around, and sees the man coming out of the dark room..."
